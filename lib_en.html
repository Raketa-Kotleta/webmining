Digital Watermark Insertion Algorithm

Authors LIJUN XIAO, WEIHONG HUANG, YONG XIE, AND KUAN-CHING LI, WEIDONG XIAO

The authors of the article propose an algorithm for protecting intellectual property (IP) copyrights using blockchain technology. The main idea is to use watermarks that are embedded in IP schemes using a quadratic transformation model and a secret sharing mechanism.

Blockchain in Copyright Management

Authors A.G. Finogeev, S.M. Vasin, L.A. Gammidulaeva

The authors propose using blockchain-based smart contract technology to reduce transaction costs and improve the efficiency of interaction in regional innovation systems. The solution ensures safe and transparent interaction between participants, automates the conclusion of contracts, registration and transfer of intellectual property rights. The system is based on the Ethereum platform, which allows creating decentralized applications and implementing innovative projects without the need to involve intermediaries. This helps optimize interaction, reduce risks and develop regional innovation ecosystems.

Article from the section "System and interactions". Federal State Budgetary Educational Institution of Higher Education Penza State University.
